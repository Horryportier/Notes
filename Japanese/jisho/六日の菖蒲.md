# 六日の菖蒲
---
1).
,六日の菖蒲 > むいかのあやめ,むいかのあやめ

- something that comes too late to be useful
- iris (blooming) on the 6th (i.e. one day too late for the Boy's Day celebration)
**( Expressions (phrases, clauses, etc.) Noun; Idiomatic expression)**
