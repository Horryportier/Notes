# 十日の菊
---
1).
,十日の菊 > とおかのきく,とおかのきく

- something that comes too late and is useless
- chrysanthemum blooming on the 10th (i.e. one day too late for the Chrysanthemum Festival)
**( Expressions (phrases, clauses, etc.) Noun; Idiomatic expression)**
