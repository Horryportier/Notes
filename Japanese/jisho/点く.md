# 点く
---
1).
,点く > つく,つく

- to be lit (of a lamp, burner, etc.)
- to catch fire
- to ignite
- to start burning
**( Godan verb with 'ku' ending Intransitive verb; Usually written using kana alone)**
> see also:  [[付く]]

,点く > つく,つく

- to be turned on (of a light, appliance, etc.)
- to come on
