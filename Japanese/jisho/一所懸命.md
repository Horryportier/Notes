# 一所懸命
---
1).
,一所懸命 > いっしょけんめい,いっしょけんめい

- very hard
- with utmost effort
- as hard as one can
- with all one's might
- for dear life
- eagerly
- desperately
**( Adverb (fukushi) Na-adjective (keiyodoshi) Noun; Yojijukugo (four character compound))**

,一所懸命 > いっしょけんめい,いっしょけんめい
