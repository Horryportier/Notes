# 机を並べる
---
1).
,机を並べる > つくえをならべる,つくえをならべる

- to work together (in the same class, workplace, etc.)
- to be classmates
- to sit side by side
- to put (one's) desks side by side
**( Expressions (phrases, clauses, etc.) Ichidan verb; Idiomatic expression)**
